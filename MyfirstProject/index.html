Welcome to Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a country in South Asia and Central Asia, bordered by Pakistan to the east and south, Iran to the west, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, and Tajikistan to the north, and China to the northeast. The country has a population of approximately 38 million people, with the majority being of Afghan ethnicity and practicing Islam. Afghanistan has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from various empires and civilizations throughout history. The official languages of the country are Dari and Pashto, although there are many other languages spoken by different ethnic groups. Afghanistan has faced many challenges, including conflict and political instability, but continues to work towards stability, security, and development.
